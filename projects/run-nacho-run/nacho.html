About

Nacho, our protagonist, is an immigrant and a family man living in the United States. He was always a kind and gentle man until the day he was deported to Mexico by ICE. Now, in order to get back to his family, he must do the unthinkable...

It is up to you to help reunite Nacho with his family. Help Nacho cross the United States - Mexico border by collecting Pesos, jumping over coyotes and evading ICE agents. Every Peso earned gets Nacho one step closer to his family.
